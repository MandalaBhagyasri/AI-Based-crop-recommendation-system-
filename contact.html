Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI recommendation?

Our AI model achieves approximately 95% accuracy based on testing with historical agricultural data. However, we always recommend consulting with local agricultural experts for final decisions.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Your agricultural data is used solely for generating recommendations and is not shared with third parties.

Can I use this system for commercial farming?

Absolutely! Our system is designed for both small-scale and commercial farming operations. The recommendations scale according to your input parameters.

How often should I get new recommendations?

We recommend getting new recommendations at the beginning of each planting season or whenever there are significant changes in your soil conditions or local climate patterns.
